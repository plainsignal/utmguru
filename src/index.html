What is a UTM Generator and UTM Builder?

A UTM generator (or UTM builder) lets you quickly add UTM parameters—such as utm_source, utm_medium, and utm_campaign—to any URL. This helps you track your campaign performance across different platforms and gain deeper insights into your marketing channels.
